UX & Information Architecture study - 2020

Information Archiectecture Redesign


The project aims to improve the user experience and findability and discoverability of information of Ischools (Information science schools). We compare and analyze the information environments of three ischools and provide critical recommendations and redesign.

My role

UX Designer and Information Architecture


Tools

Miro, Adobe XD and Adobe illustrator


Competitive analysis


First, we conducted Competitive analysis in terms of generalized topologies and audiences, general organization and content, navigation, lebeling and search system.


We also conducted a content inventory and audit of iSchool's websites by broswing all of the webpages.

Navigation Stress test


When analysing the navigation of websties, we followed Keith Instone's navigation stress test methods.



Based on the findings and IA principles, we generated the ideas about how we would redesign the navigation system.





..and there are more tasks to be done!



We will also analyze the search system if it is designed considering that the amount of content will continew to grow and adress its scalability. In addition, we will focus on the most critical redesign recommendations identified through our competitive analysis, content audit/inventory, and search analysis and design sitemaps, wireframes and some sample pages.
