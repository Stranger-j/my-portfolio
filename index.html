Subtitles for language learners

The study consists of a laboratory experiment in which non-native speakers watch subtitled videos with the playback speed dynamically adjusted based on eye movement data.

Under the supervision of Prof. Moffatt, I designed the whole project. I am conducting the first wave of experiment remotely and will proceed with the next wave in-person.

  • Supervisor: Prof. Karyn Moffatt
  • Date: From Oct 2019 to present
  • Tools: eye tracker, R, Python, Google forms
  • Research Approach: Qualitative & quantitative research
  • Research Area: Human-computer interaction, Usability, Eye-tracking, cognition
