UX research

Steam UX redesign

Despite that Steam has the highest number of active users and has been leading the game industry, its website is poorly designed. Users often feel disorientated when finding information on the website, since the information on the website is not properly organized, and too many visuals are randomly displayed. Therefore, by conducting usability testings, the project aims to evaluate the website's usability and find out better ways to organize information and provide a more intuitive and tailored experience to users.
